= 1804 Fridrich Wilhelm Weyland of Petzen no. 2 Marriage
:page-role: doc-width

In 1804 Fridrich Wilhem Weyland married Caroline Luise Lauen and Fridrich Weyland married Catharine Margaretha Drinkuth.
